Meet The Team

We are a group of Transition Year Students in Blackrock College

we each have our own passions and strenghts

but what is unique to our team is our collaboration with eachother

Darragh O'Neill

Project Manager

Darragh is our Project Manager. He is responsible for keeping the team on task through project management strategies. He is in charge of the overall execution of the project. From Social Media to CFD testing to Sponsorships. Darragh achieves this by using several project management strategies... He has an acronym for everything!

During our bi-weekly meetings Darragh creates the agenda and ensures that we stick to it. Creating short yet effective meetings. He works closely with all team members, offering assistance where necessary. Other than being our Project Manager, he is in charge of stakeholder management and ROI. Darragh is the first line of contact for our team.

Conor Blackburn

Manufacturing Engineer

Conor is our manufacturing engineer. He is responsible for advising the design team on the manufacture of the car and the constraints of the machining process. He also has a strong understanding of the parameters of the competition.

He also ensures every part of the car meets technical regulations and that the design fits within manufacturing restraints. Conor liaises with the design engineers to report and help solve any problems with the manufacturing of the car.

Fiachra Coakley

Social Media & Marketing Director

Fiachra is our Social Media & Marketing Director. He is responsible for generating our social media content. This is used to promote the team and also keep in contact with various stakeholders by "tagging" them in their respective posts.

He creates interest for the team and helps us launch sponsorship and marketing campaigns on several platforms. He is in charge of developing and maintaining our brand identity.

Philip Duffy

Finance Director

Philip is our Finance Director. He is responsible for managing the budget and allocating resources to departments. He, along with the enterprise team creates and prepares our prospectus, this is then given to Darragh to reach out and acquire our sponsors. He creates summaries of what the budget is used for, this helps sponsors with ROI.

He keeps track of all expenses and revenue generated and inputs them into our shared budget. He has a great understanding of what each department is doing, this allows him to allocate funds fairly and resourcefully.
