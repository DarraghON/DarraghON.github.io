What is
F1 in Schools

F1 in Schools is the fastest STEM competition for secondary school students! From here, you can learn how to use CAD/CAM software, to collaborate, design, analyse, manufacture, test, and then race miniature compressed air powered cars made from F1 model block.

F1 In Schools
International

This competition is massive. More than 44 countries, 20,000,000 students. And we Orbit Racing are one of these teams.
