End of Term Newsletters

To keep in contact and regurarly update sponsors and our community we have decided to produce a newsletter that will be published at the end of each school term or during mid-term breaks.

These documents will feature an update from each of our departments and aspects of our team, with special features from projects that we are involved in e.g our "Formula One in Primary Schools Project"

They will be available on our website at orbitracing.ie/newsletter, on our Instagram and via PDF which will be sent out by email and our other social media.
